Concept Basis

image embeddings you can read, compose, and slide along

An image–text embedding trained so that ~256 human-interpretable visual concepts (metallic, furry, edible, manmade…) become explicit, conditionally-orthogonal directions — a conceptual basis. Every object is representable as its profile of concept activations while the vector remains a normal retrieval embedding. The concept vocabulary is mined automatically and the training is label-free: all supervision is distilled from pretrained vision-language models. The demos below are fully static — the model's outputs over the gallery are precomputed, and your browser only does dot products.
